Within the framework of the Ponderful project, we aim to provide decision-makers involved in pond management and development policy with a decision support tool, which helps them:

In choosing a strategic option (continue as before, increase the number of ponds a little or a lot)

To have a ready-made project (with a map of all new ponds, their location, costs, etc.)

Objectives of the decision support tool:

The decision support tool builds on the scientific results of the project to help decision makers understand the advantages and disadvantages of the major policy options available to them.

The tool allows the user to contextualize the analysis by setting a number of parameters such as the choice of criteria and their weighting.

The tool has four main steps, which some steps has some sub-steps, the main steps include:

1. Selection of parameters of the zone of interest
2. Selection of decision criteria
3.Ranking the decision criteria
4.Assigning weights to the criteria
5. Selection of scenario
6. Selection of parameters for building strategic actions
7. Assigning satisfaction thresholds for each criteria
8. Run MCDA
9. Visualize results
